# The Ollie Jacob Parks PCHS STEM Scholarship Fund

A memorial website honoring Ollie Jacob Parks and supporting future Powell County STEM graduates.

🌐 **[Visit the site](https://ollieparksscholarship.org/)**

## About

The PCHS STEM Scholarship Fund was created to honor the legacy of Ollie Jacob Parks — a long-time Powell County resident whose life reflected resilience, service, and devotion to family. The fund supports Powell County High School graduates who demonstrate both STEM promise and financial need.
